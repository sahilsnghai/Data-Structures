Bubble Sort 
Time Complexity:
-Best Case: O(n)
-Average Case: O(n * n)
-Worst Case:  O(n * n)

Selection Sort 
Time Complexity:
-Best Case: O(n * n)
-Average Case: O(n * n)
-Worst Case:  O(n * n)

Insertion Sort
Time Complexity:
-Best Case: O(n)
-Average Case: O(n * n)
-Worst Case:  O(n * n)

Merge Sort
Time Complexity:
-Best Case: O(n log2(n))
-Average Case: O(n log2(n))
-Worst Case:  O(n log2(n))

Quick Sort
Time Complexity:
-Best Case: O(n log2(n))
-Average Case: O(n log2(n))
-Worst Case:  O(n * n)
